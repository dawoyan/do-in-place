── FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars) ────────────────────

Do In Place reminds you at the right moment — when you're actually there.

Instead of setting a time alarm you'll ignore, you pin a task to a place. Arrive near that place, and Do In Place reminds you. No more forgetting to pick up milk when you're already at the store, or skipping an errand because the alarm rang three hours too early.

── PLACE-BASED REMINDERS ──
Attach any reminder to a specific saved location — a store, a friend's building, your gym, your office. The app detects when you're nearby and notifies you at exactly the right moment.

── SMART SHOPPING LISTS ──
Create shopping list tasks tied to a place. Check items off as you walk through the store. Do In Place learns the order you check things in each specific store and automatically sorts your next visit accordingly. Komitas branch and Megamall have different layouts — your lists adapt to each one separately.

── SHARE TASKS WITH CONTACTS ──
Send a reminder to a trusted contact and they'll get notified when they arrive near the place. Useful for partners, family members, or housemates — "pick up the dry cleaning" goes to whoever gets there first.

── PRIORITY AND SCHEDULING ──
Mark tasks as Urgent or No Rush. Set active dates, days of the week, and time windows so reminders only fire when relevant. Tasks expire, complete, or get archived — never lost.

── ARCHIVE AND RESTORE ──
Swipe a task to archive it. It disappears from your active list but stays in your archive. Restore it any time. Undo is available for 60 seconds after every swipe.

── BUILT FOR REAL LIFE ──
• Works offline — all data is stored locally on your device
• Sync across devices with your account
• Assign tasks to others and track acceptance
• Supports Armenian, Russian, and English

Do In Place is for people who live by errands, not by clocks.
