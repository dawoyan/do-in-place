Privacy Policy

Do In Place Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Do In Place collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Do In Place Android app.

Effective date: June 9, 2026
Operator: Do In Place
Contact: do-in-place@gmail.com

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Do In Place Android application, related backend services used by the app, and support communications related to the app.

This policy is published for the Do In Place app and should be kept aligned with the live app behavior and Google Play disclosures.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, Do In Place may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account information: email address, display name, user ID, and authentication session details.
  • User-created content: tasks, reminder titles, descriptions, shopping list items, reminder schedules, task status, and related task events.
  • Saved place information: place names, addresses, latitude, longitude, radius settings, place type, and related reminder settings.
  • Location information: precise location, approximate location, background location, and geofence-related location events, when you grant those permissions.
  • Activity information: activity recognition or inferred movement context, such as walking, driving, or staying near a place, if you grant activity permission or when movement is inferred from location speed.
  • Trusted contact information: contact email, contact display name, contact user ID, invitation status, and related settings.
  • Notification information: push notification token, notification events, and in-app reminder delivery state.
  • Shared location input: text or links you intentionally share into the app from mapping apps to create or resolve a place.
  • Diagnostic and local preference data: app settings, language preference, reminder preferences, and certain local logs used for troubleshooting on your device.

3. How We Use Information

We use information processed by Do In Place to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • authenticate you and keep your session active;
  • create, sync, and update tasks, places, shopping lists, and trusted contact relationships;
  • trigger place-based reminders and geofencing behavior;
  • send notifications about reminders, task invitations, task updates, and related events;
  • improve reminder timing using location and movement context;
  • help you save a place from shared map links or search queries;
  • provide app functionality, security, debugging, and support.

4. Permissions

The app may request the following Android permissions:

  • Precise and approximate location: used to detect when you are near a saved place and to support place-based reminders.
  • Background location: used so place-based reminders can work when the app is closed.
  • Activity recognition: used to understand movement context, such as walking or driving, to reduce unnecessary reminders and improve reminder behavior.
  • Notifications: used to deliver reminders and task updates.
  • Boot completed and alarm-related capabilities: used to restore reminders after device restart and support scheduled reminders.

If you deny optional permissions, some features may not work fully, but the app is designed to continue operating with reduced functionality where possible.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in the following limited ways:

  • With service providers: to operate authentication, sync, notifications, and place search.
  • With other users you interact with: if you use trusted contacts, shared tasks, or task invitations, some task and account-related information may be visible to those users as needed for the feature to work.
  • For legal or security reasons: if required to comply with law, enforce rights, or protect users, the app, or the public.
  • With your direction: when you intentionally share content into the app or trigger features that send data to integrated services.

6. Third-Party Services

Do In Place uses third-party services that may process information on our behalf or as independent providers:

  • Supabase: authentication, backend database, and app data sync.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: push notification delivery.
  • Google Sign-In: optional sign-in method if you choose Google authentication.
  • Google Play services / Android location services: device location and geofencing support.
  • Geoapify: place search and autocomplete when you search for or resolve places.

These providers may process information according to their own privacy policies. You should review their terms and privacy notices where relevant.

7. Location and Place Search Details

Do In Place uses location and saved place coordinates to support geofencing and place-based reminders. If you search for places, your search text and, where applicable, location bias data may be sent to a place search provider to return results.

If you share a map link or location text into the app, that shared content may be parsed or resolved so the app can create a saved place.

8. Data Storage and Retention

Some information is stored locally on your device, and some information is stored in backend services used by the app so that your account and reminders can function across sessions.

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain your account, support reminders and syncing, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

Some local-only learning, preferences, and cache data may remain on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the app, or remove it through available in-app controls.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of storage, transmission, or electronic processing is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices

You may be able to:

  • grant or revoke permissions in Android system settings;
  • edit or delete tasks, places, and trusted contact relationships within the app;
  • clear local app data from your device;
  • stop using the app and uninstall it at any time.

If you need account deletion or backend data deletion support, contact: do-in-place@gmail.com.

11. Children

Do In Place is not intended for children under the age at which processing is prohibited by applicable law without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, contact us so we can review and address the issue.

12. International Processing

Depending on the providers and infrastructure used, information may be processed in countries other than your own. By using the app, you understand that information may be transferred to and processed in locations where privacy laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes should be reflected in the published policy before or at the time they take effect.
